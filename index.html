How to Set Up Your Vizio Smart TV at vizio.com/setup

1

Power On Your Vizio TV

Turn on your Vizio TV and select your preferred language and region. Make sure your TV is connected to a stable power source to avoid interruptions during setup.

2

Connect to Wi-Fi

Go to Network Settings on your TV and choose your home Wi-Fi network. Enter your Wi-Fi password carefully. A strong and stable internet connection is essential for app activation and streaming.

3

Visit vizio.com/setup

On your smartphone, tablet, or computer, open a browser and go to vizio.com/setup. Sign in with your existing Vizio account or create a new one if you’re a first-time user.

4

Enter the Setup Code

Your TV screen will display a unique setup code. Type that code into the vizio.com/setup page and click Next. This securely connects your TV to your Vizio account.

5

Complete the Activation

Follow the on-screen instructions to finish the setup. Your TV will update apps, sync your preferences, and get ready for streaming. Once done, enjoy your favorite shows and movies instantly.

Common Vizio Setup Problems and Fixes

Here are some of the most common setup and activation problems Vizio TV users face — along with quick and easy fixes to help you get back to streaming fast.

Wi-Fi Not Connecting?

Restart your Wi-Fi router and move it closer to the TV. Make sure your network password is correct and the signal is strong.

Activation Code Expired?

Refresh your TV screen to generate a new activation code and enter it again at vizio.com/setup.

Slow Performance?

Check for firmware updates in the settings menu and install them. Restart your TV after updating to improve speed.

No Sound?

Ensure HDMI ARC/eARC is enabled and check the TV’s audio output settings. Try a different HDMI port if needed.

App Not Opening?

Clear the app cache from system settings or reinstall the problematic app to fix crashes and loading issues.

Remote Not Responding?

Replace the batteries and re-pair your remote. Make sure there are no obstructions between the TV and the remote sensor.

Black Screen After Setup?

Double-check your HDMI or power cable connections. Unplug and replug the TV to perform a soft reset.

Stuck on Setup Screen?

Restart your TV and go through the setup again. If it persists, reset the TV from the settings menu.

SmartCast Not Loading?

Reset your network settings and reconnect to Wi-Fi. Restart both your TV and router before trying again.

Browser Not Loading vizio.com/setup?

Clear your browser cache and cookies, or try opening the page on a different device or browser.
